Featured Project

Just Speak Guitar Lessons

With the advancements in Voice-Controlled Technology increasing exponentially in the modern-day and making their way into our homes, accessibility, usability and convenience are at the forefront of any project. All you have to do is look around your own home! Your Fit Bit, Mobile Phone and your TV has it and things are improving with every iteration.
Inspired by a UX meetup on Voice technology advancement's and discovering that navigating a website or application vocally was just a prediction, I set my self the task to Measure The Feasibility of Vocally Navigating a Website for my University Degree Dissertation, using a non-AI voice Controlled System which resulted in a complete success. You will find this in the projects section.

  • UI UX Design

    Leveraging my design process of investigating and defining the business and users needs with user research, I then study data from User Research to ideate these expectations and a solid natural user flow for the product, create prototypes from this data drafting sketches and interactive designs using Graphics Software. These designs are then thoroughly User tested with every iteration - this process is then repeated to get the product in the best possible position before the development stage.

  • Front End Development

    Structuring and styling the designs into a website or application to optimise SEO. Fundamentally I use HTML5, CSS3 and Javascript. This ensures that the most aesthetically pleasing and secure Agile practice is used for your product. Testing the code using UX practices and correcting the mistakes in the code makes sure that the end goal of gaining complete customer satisfaction is gained.
